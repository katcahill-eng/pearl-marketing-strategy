| Document | Status | Updated |

---

## Strategic Objectives

1. **Establish Pearl as THE authority** on home performance ratings
2. **Build investor credibility** ahead of Series B
3. **Create visible market momentum** within first 90 days
4. **Articulate Pearl's moat** — 92M homes scored, proprietary methodology, first-mover data advantage, key partnerships (verify usage rights)
5. **Position Pearl as transaction infrastructure** — "Performance on Rails" messaging that emphasizes neutral mediation and Value Before Verification

---

## The Approach: Moments, Not Months

Concentrate resources around **4-6 major moments** per year rather than spreading budget thin.

- $24K spread across 12 months = invisible
- $24K concentrated in 2-3 moments = memorable

| Mar | What Buyers Want That Listings Don't Show | Spring season |

---

## Audience Priorities

**Primary:** Real estate agents, brokers, MLS executives
**Secondary:** Home buyers in transaction

---

## Partnership Leverage

Marketing's role with existing partnerships (DOE, NAR, RESO, NASEO, etc.):
- **Flag** underutilized benefits to owning divisions
- **Support** with content/collateral when they activate
- **Maximize** logo/credibility usage in marketing materials

---

## Resource Model

- **1 marketing person** — Strategy, coordination, quality control
- **AI assistance** — Content drafting, analysis, execution support
- **Outsourced execution** — PR (Atkinson), SEO (Front Page Sage), potential podcast agency
