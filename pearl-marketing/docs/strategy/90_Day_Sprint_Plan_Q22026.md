## Executive Overview

### What This Plan Achieves

Building on Q1's foundation — a complete B2C pillar series, an established B2B blog cadence, and a scaling FPS content pipeline — Q2 shifts from authority-building to activation. The April 2 data launch is the quarter's anchor moment, with spring conferences and partnership announcements providing additional surge opportunities.

**Q2 Objectives**

| Objective | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| April 2 data launch campaign | Executed with full surge |
| B2C pillar series 2 launched | 4-5 posts |
| B2B blog ramped to 2-3/month | 6-9 posts for the quarter |
| Conference campaigns (geo-fence + surge) | 2-3 events |
| FPS content pipeline sustained | 2/week delivery continuing |

### Why This Matters for Series B

Q2 is where visibility from Q1 converts into tangible market signals:
- **Data launch = product story** — First time Pearl can point to live, public data as proof of concept at scale
- **Conference presence = industry validation** — BPA, RESO, NAR events demonstrate that Pearl is embedded in the conversation
- **Content cadence = market authority** — Sustained output across B2C and B2B shows Pearl is the consistent voice in home performance
- **Partnership signals** — DOE, NAR, RESO activations signal institutional trust

### Resource Reality

Same team as Q1:
- **1 marketing person** coordinating strategy and quality
- **AI assistance** (Claude) for content creation, analysis, drafting
- **Atkinson PR** for media relations and podcast pitching
- **Front Page Sage** for B2C SEO/GEO content
- **Blogs built in Craft CMS, promoted through Sprout Social**

- "The Home Performance Gap" — what buyers expect vs. what they find

**Cadence:** Biweekly, 4-5 posts

### B2B Blog

**Theme:** Agent activation — moving from "why home performance data matters" to "how to use it"

**Target topics (adjust based on Boston offsite):**
- How to use Pearl SCORE data in listing presentations
- The agent's guide to the April 2 data launch
- What the spring conference circuit revealed about buyer expectations
- Using performance data to win listings in a competitive market
- Partnership spotlight: what DOE/NAR/RESO mean for agents
- Case study: a transaction where Pearl data made the difference

**Cadence:** 2-3/month (ramped from Q1's 1/month)

### Front Page Sage (SEO/GEO Content Partner)

**[FPS Editorial Calendar](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1sFvleRcz_DwQuSMkdOsT8PjEmNA0DxU1mUtCz0t8x04/edit?usp=sharing)** (FPS-managed — view only)

**Strategy:** FPS produces SEO- and GEO-optimized content targeting consumer search queries related to home performance. Content is structured around keyword hubs to build topical authority. FPS also places AI Exchange Q+A articles on external sites to strengthen Pearl's authority signals in LLM citation results.

**Q2 Deliverables:**
