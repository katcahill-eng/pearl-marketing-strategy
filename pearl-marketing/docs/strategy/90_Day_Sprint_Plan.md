## Executive Overview

### What This Plan Achieves

In 90 days, this plan establishes Pearl as a visible, credible authority in the real estate market—creating the momentum the board needs to see ahead of Series B.

**Q1 Objectives**

| Objective | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Pillar content pieces with media coverage | 3 |
| Major industry speaking events | 2+ |
| Podcast appearances secured | 12-18 |
| Geo-fence campaigns (of 10 annual) | 1 |

### Why This Matters for Series B

Investors need to see:
- **Press coverage** — Proof the market is paying attention
- **Industry adoption signals** — Sponsored speaking slots, partnership references, recorded presentations
- **Pearl SCORE becoming a recognized term** — Search visibility, social mentions
- **Clear moat articulation** — 92M homes scored, proprietary methodology, first-mover data advantage, key partnerships (verify usage rights in Q1 audit)

This plan front-loads visibility activities to create tangible evidence of market momentum.

### Resource Reality

This plan is designed for:
- **1 marketing person** coordinating strategy and quality
- **AI assistance** (Claude) for content creation, analysis, drafting
- **Atkinson PR** for media relations and podcast pitching
- **Front Page Sage** for B2C SEO content (consumer-focused blog posts)
- **~$50K/month variable budget** (adjust activities if budget differs)

## Key Activities Detail

### 1. "State of Home Performance 2026" Report

**What:** Flagship annual report establishing Pearl as the definitive source on home performance trends.

**Contents:**
- National home performance trends by region
- What buyers are asking that listings don't show
- The rise of performance-aware buyers
- Pearl SCORE methodology highlights
- 2026 predictions

**Execution:**
- AI drafts initial content from Pearl data + market research
- Marketing edits and refines
- Design via Canva or contractor
- Gate behind email capture
- Coordinate launch with Atkinson for media push

---

### 2. Geo-Fencing Campaigns

**What:** Display/video ads served to devices within conference venue radius.

**Events:**
- Inman Connect NYC (Feb 3-5)

**Execution:**
- Work with geo-fencing vendor ($6-7K per event)
- Creative: Pearl SCORE awareness, report download CTA
- Retarget engaged users for 30 days post-event

---

### 3. Podcast Strategy

**Goal:** 12-18 appearances in 90 days (4-6/month)

**Approach:**
- Atkinson pitching 12/month (included in retainer)
- Consider podcast agency for additional 20-30 pitches ($1,500-2,500/month)
- Focus on real estate and home/lifestyle shows
- Rotate spokespeople based on topic fit

**Talking Points (for all appearances):**
- Pearl built first national home performance rating system
- 92M homes scored across 5 pillars
- Buyers asking questions listings don't answer
- pearlscore.com - look up any home

**NEW: "Performance on Rails" Messaging (Q1 2026):**
- "When two non-experts debate expert subjects during a home purchase, the conversation itself becomes the risk. Pearl puts those conversations on rails."
- "Pearl helps buyers and sellers get on the same page—before commitment and pressure peak."
- "We're not here to help one side win. We're here to create common ground."
- "Pearl is transaction infrastructure—we facilitate deals by replacing debates with structured discovery."

---

### 4. Speaker Support Kit

**What:** One document that helps anyone presenting on Pearl's behalf.

**Contents:**
- Pearl story (2-minute version)
- **NEW: "Performance on Rails" positioning and clarifying statement**
- Key statistics
- The 5 pillars explained
- "Why now" narrative
- **NEW: Value Before Verification principle**
- Common questions + answers
- Slides template
- Demo script for pearlscore.com

**Key Message to Include:**
> "The Pearl app helps buyers and sellers get on the same page, prioritize what's most important, and achieve clarity on a home's performance—before commitment and pressure peak."

**Use:** Share with founders, BD, anyone speaking at conferences or on podcasts.

---

### 5. Video Recording Requirements

**Strategy:** Record ALL sponsored speaking presentations to build a speaking bureau portfolio for 2027.

**Recording Requirements:**
- Professional quality video (1080p minimum)
- Clear audio capture (lapel mic preferred)
- Speaker + slides visible
- Audience Q&A captured when possible

**Confirm with each conference sponsor package:**
- [ ] Recording rights included?
- [ ] Conference provides recording, or must we hire?
- [ ] When will recording be delivered?
- [ ] Usage rights for speaking bureau submissions?

**2027 Speaking Bureau Goal:** Use 2026 recordings to apply for paid keynote speaking circuits in 2027.
