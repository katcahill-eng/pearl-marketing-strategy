# Partnership Benefits: Marketing Opportunities Assessment
## Supporting Pearl's Authority Positioning

**Purpose:** Identify partnership benefits that support the marketing goal of establishing Pearl as THE authority on home performance ratings—without stepping on other divisions' responsibilities.

**Approach:**
- **Marketing Pushes** = Benefit owned by another division; marketing advocates for them to use it
- **Marketing Supports** = Benefit execution needs marketing help (content, messaging, collateral)
- **Marketing Leads** = Benefit is directly marketing-relevant; we should own execution

---

## Strategic Filter

For each benefit, ask: **Does this help establish Pearl as the leading authority on home performance ratings?**

Relevant activities:
- Speaking to real estate / policy audiences
- Media and thought leadership placement
- Content distribution to target audiences
- Logo/credibility signals for marketing materials
- Access to decision makers who influence adoption

NOT marketing's focus (but may support):
- Technical training for contractors
- Program administration
- Policy advocacy (unless public-facing)
- Sales/BD relationship building

## Interview Questions for Partnership Owners

When meeting with each division:

1. What does this partnership actually include? (Get the membership agreement if possible)
2. What benefits have we used in the past 12 months?
3. What benefits have we NOT used?
4. Are there speaking opportunities? Who submits?
5. Are there logo/badge rights? What are the guidelines?
6. Are there content placement opportunities (newsletters, publications)?
7. What would help you take better advantage of this partnership?
8. Would marketing support (content, slides, messaging) be helpful?
