## Naming Conventions

- **Pearl SCORE™** — always all caps SCORE with trademark symbol
- **Pearl for professionals** — official app name, lowercase "for" and "professionals"
- **Pearl app for professionals** — acceptable in body copy
- **Pearl** — consumer app name
- **Pearl app** — acceptable in body copy, lowercase "app"
- **Pearl Home Performance Registry** — capitalize all words
- **Pearl SCORE™ Report** — the report product
- **Pearl Listing Package** — capitalize Pearl and Listing Package
- **Pearl Academy** — capitalize both words

## Key Messaging Rules

- Buyer agents always come first, seller agents second
- Never frame SCORE as helping sellers make more money
- Never say "backed by" DOE — only "in partnership with"
- Pearl creates understanding, not decisions
- The SCORE belongs to the home, not to either party
- No pricing language in any product copy — handled inside the apps
