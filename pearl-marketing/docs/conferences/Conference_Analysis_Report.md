# Pearl Conference Analysis Report
## Real Estate Market Impact Assessment

**Analysis Date:** January 15, 2026
**Data Source:** Conferences, Webinars, & Events.xlsx (2022-2026)

---

## Executive Summary

### Key Finding
Pearl's historical conference strategy has been heavily weighted toward **energy efficiency** and **contractor** audiences. For the 2026 marketing strategy focused on establishing authority with **real estate professionals**, the conference mix needs significant rebalancing.

### Recommendation
Shift investment toward real estate-focused events (Inman, NAR, T3 Sixty, RESO) while maintaining selective presence at high-value energy/contractor events that have real estate crossover potential.
