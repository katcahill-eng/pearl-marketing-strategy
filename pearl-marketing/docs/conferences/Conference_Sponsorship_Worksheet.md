# Conference Sponsorship & Paid Opportunities Worksheet

Use this worksheet to research and document paid sponsorship, speaking, and exhibit opportunities at target conferences. This informs budget decisions and identifies high-value investments.

---

## 2026 Strategy: Sponsored Speaking Only

**Key Principle:** Pearl is NOT pursuing unpaid speaking opportunities. All conference speaking must come through sponsorship packages that include:
1. **Guaranteed speaking slot** (mainstage, breakout, or panel)
2. **Recording rights** for all presentations

**Why:** Recordings from 2026 sponsored speaking slots will be used to build a speaking bureau portfolio for 2027, enabling Pearl to secure paid keynote opportunities.

---

## Research Instructions

For each conference, contact the organizer or review their sponsorship prospectus to complete:
1. Available sponsorship tiers and costs
2. Speaking slot costs (if paid)
3. Booth/exhibit costs
4. Add-on opportunities (dinners, receptions, etc.)
5. **Video recording rights and delivery timeline**
