# Pearl Early Access Program (EAP) — Reference

**Source:** [EAP Pitch Deck](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1a6E9x9Kpywd7UfMM2eV_aRXSiiIW-Vxg_ScsfEEO6T8/edit) (Google Slides, March 2026)
**Presenter:** Paul Bosson, VP Channel Partnerships

## What It Is

The Early Access Program invites a small group of real estate agents to help shape how Pearl is introduced into real estate. Agents use Pearl tools in buyer and listing conversations, help refine workflows, and provide field feedback.

## Problem Being Solved

- Buyers shop on half the story — listings show price, square footage, location, finishes, but not operating costs, efficiency, comfort, resilience, or maintenance
- After moving in, homeowners discover hidden costs: high utility bills, uneven temperatures, surprise insurance increases, unclear upgrade priorities
- Homeownership costs are increasingly unpredictable:
  - Only 17% of first-time buyers can afford a median-priced home
  - Utility/energy costs up 35% since 2022
  - Average non-mortgage costs: $24K/year
  - Insurance premiums up 58% since 2018
  - Average buyer is 40, purchasing a 40-year-old home
  - Sellers outnumber buyers by 500K; 27% of homes have reduced price

## What Agents Get

- Access to Pearl tools and workflows
- Marketing templates and content for social media and SOI
- Discounts on Pearl reports for buyers and sellers
- A new dimension for client conversations (performance beyond aesthetics)
- Long-term advisor positioning (not just transaction partner)
- Co-branded Pearl resources for post-transaction homeowner engagement

## What Agents Do

- Use Pearl tools in buyer and listing conversations
- Help refine agent workflows and resources
- About 1 hour/month of feedback (surveys, interviews, focus groups)
- Test Pearl tools with clients and SOI
- Honest input on what works and what doesn't

## Agent Value Proposition

Agents can:
- Show hidden value of home upgrades for sellers
- Explain operating costs and efficiency in simple terms
- Provide buyers with insights beyond listing photos (via Pearl SCORE reports)
- Tell the complete story of a home for sellers
- Stay connected to clients post-transaction as long-term advisors

## Key Pearl Context (from deck)

- Pearl SCORE is like FICO for homes — single standardized metric from 700+ data points
- 5 pillars: Safety, Comfort, Operations, Resilience, Energy (200 pts each, 1000 total)
- 93M+ single-family homes rated
- Aligns with DOE, RESNET, EPA, BPI, NFPA standards
- Two-pronged go-to-market: agent recruitment/enablement + data partnerships/MLS integration
- Pearl Home Performance Registry (Beta) — public, searchable, homeowners can claim homes
