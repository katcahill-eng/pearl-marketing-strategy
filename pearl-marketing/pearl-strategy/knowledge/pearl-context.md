# Pearl Strategic Context

## Mission
Make home performance matter.

## Products

### Pearl SCORE
Rates every US single-family home on a 1-1000 scale across 5 pillars:
- **Safety** — structural integrity, fire risk, environmental hazards
- **Comfort** — HVAC, insulation, air quality, noise
- **Operations** — maintenance status, systems age, repair history
- **Resilience** — disaster preparedness, backup systems, climate adaptation
- **Energy** — efficiency ratings, solar, electrification, utility costs

### Pearl Home Performance Registry
The first comprehensive database covering 92+ million US single-family homes. No competitor has national-scale home performance data at this breadth.

### Pearl App
Consumer homeowner app (React Native) that surfaces SCORE data, helps homeowners understand and improve their home's performance, and connects them with certified contractors.

## Target Markets

### B2B
- Real estate agents — use SCORE in listings, buyer consultations, and market differentiation
- Contractors and home service providers — lead generation, certification, performance verification
- Home inspectors — enhanced reporting and data integration
- MLS/proptech platforms — data licensing and API integration

### B2C
- Homeowners — understand home performance, prioritize improvements, track value
- Buyers — evaluate homes beyond cosmetics, compare performance across listings
- Sellers — showcase home investments, justify pricing, differentiate listings

## Core Messaging (Q1 2026)
**"Performance on Rails"** — The Pearl app helps buyers and sellers get on the same page, prioritize what's most important, and achieve clarity on a home's performance — before commitment and pressure peak.

## Competitive Position
**First mover in national home performance scoring.** Competitors:
- **RealReports** — property reports for agents, limited to disclosure data
- **Shipshape AI** — home maintenance tracking, no scoring or national data
- **HouseCanary** — valuation analytics, not performance-focused

None have Pearl's national scale, 5-pillar comprehensive approach, or contractor certification network.
