# Terminology Rules

- First reference: "Pearl SCORE(TM)"; subsequent: "Pearl SCORE" or "the SCORE"
- Never use SCORE as a verb
- Pillars always in order: Safety, Comfort, Operations, Resilience, Energy
- Free consumer product: "Pearl Snapshot" via "Pearl Home Performance Registry(TM)"
- Scale: 1-1,000 (not 0-1,000)
