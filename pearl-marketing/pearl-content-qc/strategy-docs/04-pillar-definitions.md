# Pillar Definitions

Pillar confusion is the #1 error in Pearl content. Features must be assigned to the correct pillar.

## The Five Pillars (always in this order)

1. **SAFETY:** Health hazards — indoor air quality, radon, carbon monoxide, mold, hazardous materials, water quality, accessibility
2. **COMFORT:** Livability — temperature consistency, humidity control, drafts, acoustic comfort, daylighting
3. **OPERATIONS:** Running costs — HVAC efficiency, appliances, building envelope (insulation, air sealing, ductwork), water systems, maintenance requirements
4. **RESILIENCE:** Stress readiness — extreme weather, natural disasters, power outages, climate stress, backup systems
5. **ENERGY:** Modern energy — solar panels, battery storage, smart devices, EV readiness, grid integration

## Critical Rule

HVAC, insulation, building envelope, air sealing, and ductwork are **OPERATIONS** features. They are **NOT** Energy features.
