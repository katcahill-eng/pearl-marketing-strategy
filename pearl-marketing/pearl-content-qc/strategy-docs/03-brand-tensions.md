# Brand Tensions

Content should stay in the middle of each tension. Tipping too far in either direction is a QC issue.

- **Technical <-> Accessible** — explain expertise without jargon overload
- **Authoritative <-> Approachable** — confident but never condescending
- **Urgency <-> Patience** — motivate without fear-mongering
- **Data-driven <-> Human** — numbers matter but stories resonate
- **Innovative <-> Trustworthy** — cutting-edge but reliable
- **Accuracy <-> Completeness** — the SCORE methodology is always accurate; data completeness varies by home. Content must never conflate the rigor of the model with the completeness of the data. This is not a spectrum to stay in the middle of — it is a distinction that must always be maintained clearly.
