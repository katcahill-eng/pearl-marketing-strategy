# Approved Statistical Claims

## Pearl Buyer Research (sample: 800 surveyed buyers)

- 80%+ of buyers want specific performance information and would value a single standardized metric
- 79% would use performance information to guide questions for their inspector
- 90% say safety factors influence their purchase decision
- 47% are already concerned about the accuracy of home profile data
- Operating costs ranked last as a purchase driver

## Pearl Agent Research (March 2026, 11 agents interviewed)

- Agents confirmed demand for structured performance framework
- One agent said he would use it in every listing packet

## Framing Rules for Statistics

- Safety ranks FIRST among buyer performance concerns — lead with safety, comfort, confidence
- Operating costs rank LAST as purchase driver — never lead with cost savings
- Buyers fear unexpected capital events (e.g., $60,000 roof replacement), not monthly utility differences
- A $50/month utility difference is manageable and predictable; a major unplanned repair is not
