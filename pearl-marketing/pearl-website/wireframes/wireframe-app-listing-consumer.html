Detailed Description

Copy as it will appear in the store (line breaks, no HTML):

The way homes are bought and sold is changing. 90% of buyers say safety and health factors influence their purchase decision — but until now, there hasn't been a way to evaluate how comfortable a home is, what it costs to run, or how well its systems hold up over time. Pearl changes that. Benefits for buyers: ◆ Discover the five factors that shape what a home is actually like to own ◆ View a home performance profile for any single-family home in the U.S. — the nation's first searchable registry ◆ Save favorites and compare homes side by side ◆ Order a Pearl SCORE™ Report on active listings to narrow your shortlist Benefits for sellers: ◆ Claim your home and add the upgrades a traditional listing can't capture — so buyers see the real home, not just the public record ◆ Build a more complete performance profile that sets your home apart in the market ◆ Find rebates and tax credits for home energy improvements based on your location ◆ Store and access important home documents in one place Pearl works in partnership with the U.S. Department of Energy, the National Association of REALTORS®, the Appraisal Institute, and RESO.
