Detailed Description

Copy as it will appear in the store (line breaks, no HTML):

The way homes are bought and sold is changing. 90% of buyers say safety and health factors influence their purchase decision. Home performance is already on their mind — Pearl puts it in your hands. Pearl for professionals puts everything you need to evaluate, compare, and explain home performance into one workspace. Help buyers understand what a home is really like to own before they commit. Help sellers showcase upgrades that a traditional listing can't capture. Key features: ◆ Discover why home performance is reshaping the way homes are bought and sold ◆ View Pearl SCORE™ Reports for any active single-family listing — searchable by address ◆ Help buyers compare homes on their shortlist ◆ Claim homes, update features, and generate Pearl Listing Packages for your selling clients ◆ Access Pearl Academy — learn to lead home performance conversations Pearl works in partnership with the U.S. Department of Energy, the National Association of REALTORS®, the Appraisal Institute, and RESO.
