WHAT IS PEARL SCORE
What is Pearl SCORE

A home's performance, finally quantified.

Every home has hidden performance characteristics that affect your comfort, your utility bills, and your long-term investment. Until now, there's been no easy way to see them.

The Pearl SCORE Report translates your home's features — systems, materials, equipment, and certifications — into a clear numeric score and letter grade trusted by appraisers, lenders, and real estate professionals nationwide.

🌡️
Heating & Cooling
HVAC efficiency, equipment age, duct performance, and smart controls.
💨
Indoor Air Quality
Ventilation systems, filtration, and healthy home features.
🏠
Building Envelope
Insulation, windows, air sealing, and the shell that holds it all together.
⚡
Energy & Renewables
Solar, batteries, EV charging, and grid-interactive features.
💧
Water Efficiency
Efficient fixtures, tankless systems, and smart irrigation.
📋
Certifications
ENERGY STAR, LEED, Pearl Certification, and other verified programs.

How buyers get a report

HOW IT WORKS — HOMEOWNERS / SELLERS
For Homeowners & Sellers

How homeowners build their report

Already own your home? Add your home's features and upgrades directly in the Pearl App to generate your official SCORE Report — then share it when you're ready to sell.

1
Create your account
Download the Pearl App and set up your homeowner account.
2
Claim your home
Enter your address. Pearl pulls available public data to start your profile automatically.
3
Add your home's assets
Log your high-performance features — HVAC upgrades, solar, insulation, windows, certifications, and more — directly in the app.
4
Get & share your report
Receive your Pearl SCORE Report with a numeric score, letter grade, and full breakdown — ready to share with buyers and agents.
