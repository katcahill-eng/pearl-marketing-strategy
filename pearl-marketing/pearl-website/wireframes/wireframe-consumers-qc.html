WHAT IS PEARL SCORE
Beyond the Listing
How a home lives matters.

The things that impact everyday life in a home — whether it stays comfortable year-round, what it costs to run every month, how its systems hold up season after season — have never been organized into one place so that buyers and sellers can actually compare them. Over 200 years of combined building science expertise went into finding that data across utility records, public filings, and building specs — and organizing it into five critical performance pillars that make home performance part of the home transaction for the first time.

App Screenshot
SCORE Report overview
showing overall score
S
Safety
How well does this home protect against health hazards like mold, radon, carbon monoxide, poor air quality, and contaminated water?
C
Comfort
Can this home maintain proper temperature and humidity year-round? How well insulated is it? Does it protect occupants from outdoor noise?
O
Operations
How efficiently do the home's systems work together? This includes HVAC performance, water heating, appliance efficiency, and smart home integration.
R
Resilience
Is this home prepared for major weather events like flooding, wildfires, hurricanes, or severe winter storms?
E
Energy
What are the home's energy generation and storage capabilities? This covers solar panels, battery backup, EV charging, and overall energy consumption.

FOR SELLERS
For Sellers
The market sees your home one way. Is it the whole story?

The market sees your home one way — built from public records and listing data. But that version doesn't include the upgrades you've made. The Pearl SCORE™ Report combines public data with information you provide to tell a more complete story — so buyers can truly appreciate what sets your home apart.

  • Claim your home inside Pearl app and add the upgrades only you know about — insulation, efficient HVAC, solar, and more
  • Build a Pearl SCORE™ Report that reflects the real home, not just the public record
  • Make sure your home's full story is part of the conversation from the start
